# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in scrub.txt a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Helping newcomers get started

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
